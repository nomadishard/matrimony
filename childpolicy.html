Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Introduction

Welcome to our dedicated page on Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). At Kokani rishta, we prioritize the safety and well-being of children in the digital space. Our commitment to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation is reflected in our comprehensive policies and practices.

Our CSAE Standards

  • User Reporting Mechanisms: Users can report any suspected CSAE content directly within our app. Reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly to ensure swift action against violations.
  • Child Safety Policies: We adhere to strict policies that prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation. These policies are designed to create a safe environment for all users.
  • Compliance with Legal Requirements: Our app complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, ensuring that we meet or exceed legal standards for protecting children online.

Implementation of Standards

To effectively implement these standards, we:

  • Educate Users: Provide resources and information to help users understand the importance of reporting suspicious activities.
  • Regular Audits: Conduct regular audits of our systems and policies to ensure compliance with our CSAE standards.
  • Collaboration with Authorities: Work closely with law enforcement and child protection agencies to report incidents of CSAE promptly.

Feedback Mechanism

We value feedback from our users. If you have any suggestions or concerns regarding our CSAE policies, please contact us through our feedback mechanism available in the app.

Resources

For more information on child protection online, you can visit:

  • Google Play Child Safety Standards
  • CyberTipline - Missing Kids
  • UNICEF Guidelines on Child Online Protection

Conclusion

At Kokani rishta, we are committed to fostering a safe digital environment for children. By adhering to our CSAE standards, we aim to prevent abuse and exploitation, ensuring that children can navigate the online world safely.
